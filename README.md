## 📊 Corporate Income Statement Dashboard

### 🎯 Project Overview

A comprehensive financial analytics dashboard engineered to track and evaluate corporate fiscal performance over multiple fiscal years. This tool provides finance teams and executives with an interactive view of revenue streams, expense breakdowns, profit margins, and key earnings-per-share (EPS) metrics to drive strategic planning.

---

### 🛠️ Tech Stack

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Core development environment for data visualization and financial reporting layout.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Implemented for structural reshaping of relational accounting tables and ledger records.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used to compute complex financial metrics, year-over-year targets, and performance variances.

---

### 📂 Data Source

* **Source:** Internal Corporate ERP & General Ledger databases.
* **Structure:** Multi-year financial data containing detailed line items for revenues, cost of goods sold (COGS), operating expenses (OpEx), tax provisions, and share counts.

---

### 💡 Features & Highlights

#### • Business Problem

Reviewing traditional, static income statements makes it difficult for executives to quickly grasp historical performance trends, identify structural cost drivers, or see how closely the company is hitting its strategic profit targets.

#### • Goal of the Dashboard

* Provide an immediate operational snapshot of key organizational profitability tiers (Gross, Operating, and Net).
* Enable rapid benchmarking against corporate fiscal goals.
* Visualize the structural breakdown of expenses and revenue flows over time.

##### 📉 Historical Performance: Total Revenue by FY (Middle Right Chart)

* Interactive tab buttons allowing users to toggle the lower visual canvas dynamically between **Expenditure Breakup**, **Total Revenue by FY**, **EPS Trend**, and **Total Revenue Breakup**.

##### 📊 Total Revenue Breakup (Lower Right Funnel/Flow Chart)

* A specialized horizontal breakdown visual clarifying how revenue flows down into profits and costs:
* **Total Revenue** normalized at 100%.
* **Gross Margin:** $135.62K$ ($68\%$).

#### • Business Impact & Insights

* **Target Accountability:** Executive teams can instantly confirm that the organization outperformed all three core fiscal goals by over 17%.
* **Expense Leakage Controls:** Visualizing total cost of revenue ($62.65K$ or $32\%$) alongside other expenses ($46.34K$ or $23\%$) helps controllers monitor margin compression.
* **Investor Relations Readiness:** Tracking the growth of basic EPS from **$8** in FY21 to **$9.7** in FY22 simplifies reporting for stakeholders.
